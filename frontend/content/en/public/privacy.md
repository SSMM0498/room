# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** November 28, 2025

room_ (the "Company", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to maintaining robust privacy protections for its users. Our Privacy Policy ("Privacy Policy") is designed to help you understand how we collect, use and safeguard the information you provide to us and to assist you in making informed decisions when using our Service.

## Definitions

For purposes of this Privacy Policy:

**"Site"** refers to the Company's website and platform, which can be accessed at room_.

**"Service"** refers to the Company's educational platform services accessed via the Site, including all features for Trainers and Students.

**"Personal Information"** means information that can be used to personally identify you, such as your name, email address, payment information, and other data you provide to us.

**"Non-Personal Information"** means information that cannot be used to personally identify you, such as anonymous usage data, general demographic information, referring/exit pages and URLs, platform types, preferences, and aggregated statistics.

**"Trainer"** refers to users who create and publish educational content on the platform.

**"Student"** refers to users who access and consume educational content on the platform.

**"You"** or **"User"** refers to you, as a user of our Site or our Service, whether as a Trainer, Student, or visitor.

By accessing our Site or our Service, you accept our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service, and you consent to our collection, storage, use and disclosure of your Personal Information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access or use our Service.

## I. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect both Non-Personal Information and Personal Information to provide and improve our Service.

### 1. Personal Information

We collect Personal Information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

- **Register for an account:** We collect your name, email address, username, and password.
- **Create a profile:** Trainers may provide additional information such as bio, ((expertise areas)), profile photo, and social media links.
- **Make a purchase:** We collect billing information including name, payment card details (processed securely through third-party payment processors), billing address, and transaction history.
- **Upload content:** Trainers provide course materials, videos, documents, and other educational content.
- **Communicate with us:** When you contact our support team, we collect your email address, name, and the content of your messages.
- **Participate in surveys or promotions:** We may collect additional information you choose to provide.

### 2. Information Collected Automatically via Technology

To activate and use the Service, we automatically collect certain information about your device and how you interact with our Service:

**Device and Browser Information:**
- Browser type and version
- Operating system
- Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)
- Screen resolution
- IP address
- Time zone settings
- ((Referring/exit URLs))
- Date and time of access

**Usage Information:**
- Pages viewed and content accessed
- Course enrollment and completion data
- Video watch time and progress
- Search queries within the platform
- Clickstream data
- Features used and interactions with the platform

**Cookies and Tracking Technologies:**

We use cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies to collect information about your use of our Service. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us:

- Keep you logged in
- Remember your preferences
- Understand user behavior and improve our Service
- Provide personalized content recommendations
- Analyze trends and usage patterns

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. However, disabling cookies may limit your ability to use certain features of our Service.

**Types of Cookies We Use:**

- **Essential Cookies:** Necessary for the operation of our Service
- **Analytics Cookies:** Help us understand how users interact with our Service
- **Functional Cookies:** Remember your preferences and settings
- **Advertising Cookies:** Used to deliver relevant advertisements (if applicable)

### 3. User-Generated Content

We collect and store content that you create and upload to the platform, including:

- **For Trainers:** Course materials, videos, documents, presentations, course descriptions, and any other educational content
- **For Students:** Course reviews, ratings, comments,  contributions to discussions
- **For All Users:** Profile information, messages, and communications within the platform

### 4. Payment Information

When you make a purchase on our platform, payment information is collected and processed by our third-party payment processors. We do not store complete credit card information on our servers. We may retain limited payment information such as the last four digits of your card, card type, and transaction history for record-keeping and customer service purposes.

### 5. Information from Third-Party Services

If you choose to link your account with third-party services (such as social media platforms or single sign-on providers), we may receive information from those services, including:

- Profile information (name, email, profile picture)
- Friends or connections list (if you grant permission)
- Other information you authorize those services to share

## IV. HOW WE PROTECT YOUR INFORMATION

We take the security of your Personal Information seriously and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect it from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

### Security Measures Include:

**Technical Safeguards:**
- Encryption of data in transit using SSL/TLS technology
- Encryption of sensitive data at rest
- Secure authentication and access controls
- Regular security assessments and penetration testing
- Firewalls and intrusion detection systems
- Secure software development practices

**Organizational Safeguards:**
- Limited access to Personal Information on a need-to-know basis
- Employee training on data protection and privacy
- Confidentiality agreements with employees and contractors
- Incident response and breach notification procedures
- Regular security audits and compliance reviews

**Account Security:**

Your account is protected by a password that you create. We strongly recommend that you:

- Choose a strong, unique password
- Keep your password confidential
- Do not share your account credentials with others
- Log out of your account after each session, especially on shared devices
- Enable two-factor authentication if available
- Notify us immediately of any unauthorized access to your account

### Limitations

Despite our security measures, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

By using our Service, you acknowledge that you understand and agree to assume these risks. If we learn of a security breach, we will notify you in accordance with applicable law so that you can take appropriate protective steps.

You have the right to request access to the Personal Information we hold about you. You can access and review much of your information by logging into your account.

You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your Personal Information under certain circumstances.

Please note that even if you opt out of promotional communications, we will still send you transactional and administrative emails related to your account and purchases.

### Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at [contact@roomcursor.com](mailto:contact@roomcursor.com). We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe and in accordance with applicable law. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

## X. COOKIES POLICY

### What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our Site. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and understanding how you use our Service.

### How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

**Essential Cookies:** These cookies are necessary for the operation of our Service. They enable core functionality such as security, authentication, and accessibility.

**Performance and Analytics Cookies:** These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our Service by collecting and reporting information anonymously. We use tools like Google Analytics to analyze usage patterns.

**Functional Cookies:** These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization, such as remembering your preferences and settings.

**Advertising Cookies:** These cookies may be used to deliver relevant advertisements and track the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns.

### Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

- View what cookies are stored on your device
- Delete all or specific cookies
- Block cookies from specific websites
- Block all cookies
- Delete all cookies when you close your browser

Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our Service and limit your user experience.

For more information about managing cookies, visit [www.allaboutcookies.org](http://www.allaboutcookies.org).

### Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our Site are placed by third-party services. We do not control these cookies or how third parties use them. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these third parties for more information.

## XI. DO NOT TRACK SIGNALS

Some web browsers have a "Do Not Track" (DNT) feature that signals to websites that you do not want to be tracked. Currently, there is no industry standard for how to respond to DNT signals.

At this time, our Service does not respond to DNT signals. If a standard for responding to DNT signals is established in the future, we will update this Privacy Policy accordingly.
